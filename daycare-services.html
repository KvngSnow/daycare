Daycare Services for Ages 0-5

At Marvellous Kids Childcare Ltd, we specialize in providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for children aged 0-5. Our goal is to support your child’s growth, learning, and development during these critical early years.

What We Offer:

  1. Infant Care (0-2 years): Gentle, loving care tailored to your baby’s individual needs, focusing on sensory play and emotional security.
  2. Toddler Care (2-3 years): Engaging activities that promote language development, motor skills, and social interaction
  3. Preschool Program (3-5 years): Structured learning through play to prepare your child for school, including numeracy, literacy, and problem-solving activities.

Our Approach

We understand that parents need reliable and consistent support when it comes to childcare. At Marvellous Kids Childcare, our team goes above and beyond to provide you with peace of mind:

Play-Based Learning:

We believe children learn best through play. Our activities are designed to foster creativity, curiosity, and confidence.

Experienced Team:

Our caregivers are highly trained, DBS-checked, and passionate about early childhood education.

Healthy Meals:

Nutritious meals and snacks are provided daily, catering to all dietary needs.

Free Funded Places

We are proud to offer free tax-funded childcare for 2, 3, and 4-year-olds, making high-quality childcare accessible to all families.

At Marvellous Kids Childcare Ltd, we are dedicated to helping your little ones thrive in a loving and supportive environment. Contact us today to learn more or book a visit!
